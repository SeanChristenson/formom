Over the past several years, rising costs throughout the Flathead Valley have left working- and middle-class families scrambling to keep up with spiraling property taxes and never ending increases in the cost of food, insurance, transportation, and housing; leaving many families struggling just to get by. If you cannot afford the rising cost of heat and need firewood to help keep your family warm through the winter, we are here to help.

Cords for Care offers free and low-cost fire mitigation work throughout the Flathead Valley. We use the collected timber to create firewood, which we offer to the community free of charge. If you need help, reach out.
