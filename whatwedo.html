When coming up with our tagline, we had a LOT of ideas. Some were funny. Some were serious. Most we discarded, but one really stuck with us. It wasn’t a good one. In fact, it was pretty bad, but it was honest. The discarded tagline was “Because 4 th of July is coming and so is -8.” It was said as a joke, but it is true.

Summer is coming. It will be dry and hot. Tens of thousands of tourists will stream into Montana. There will be campfires, fireworks, thunderstorms and lightning, and there will be forest fires.

Winter is coming. There will be snow, ice, wind, and freezing rain. Temperatures will plummet and some of our neighbors won’t have enough heat.

We know these things to be certain. We also know that fire mitigation clearing reduces the size and impact of fires as surely as firewood helps to keep homes warm. That’s why we do both.
