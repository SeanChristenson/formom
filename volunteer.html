People who are willing to offer their time to better their communities are always more than welcome at Cords for Care.

By volunteering with us, you can support your neighbors and your community, while helping to make the Flathead Valley a safer place during fire season. Whether it is delivering firewood, felling a tree, or organizing a fundraiser, we have a place for you.

If you are interested in volunteering for our organization, please fill out the form to the right and let us know what you are most interested in. We will get back to you with a follow-up email and further steps.
